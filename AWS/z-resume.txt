Develop strategies for optimizing cloud resource utilization and reducing operational overhead.
Cloud Strategy & Planning:
Collaborate with key stakeholders to design and refine cloud strategies tailored to the diverse needs of real estate, healthcare, and financial services.

Continuous Improvement & Best Practices:
Stay up to date with the latest trends and technologies in cloud computing, network architecture, and infrastructure management.
Recommend and implement industry best practices to enhance infrastructure resilience, security, and efficiency.
Lead initiatives to continuously improve and innovate the infrastructure to align with evolving business goals.

Design and implement data replication strategies across multiple AWS regions.
